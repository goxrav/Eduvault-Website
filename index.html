Features

Everything You Need to Study Smarter

EduVault is packed with powerful features to make accessing and sharing study materials seamless for every student.

Secure Authentication

Powered by Firebase Auth, your account and data are protected with industry-standard security and encrypted sign-in.

File Upload & Sharing

Instantly upload PDFs, images, and documents. Share your best notes with classmates across departments and semesters.

Organized Notes

Materials are neatly categorized by subject and semester, so you always find exactly what you need without the clutter.

Search & Filter

Powerful search and filtering lets you pin-point notes by keyword, subject, semester, or file type in seconds.

View & Access

Browse and view study materials instantly within the app. Access your study resources anytime, anywhere — right from your device.

Cross-Platform

Built with React Native and Expo, EduVault runs smoothly on both Android and iOS with a single, unified codebase.
